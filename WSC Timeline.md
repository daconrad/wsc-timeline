## **2001**

**WSC Established** 

The WSC is planned in 2000 and established in 2001 by a diverse group of conservation minded anglers due to closures of North Puget Sound Streams, including the Skagit.

**WSC Publishes Biological & Economic Benefits of Wild Steelhead Release Paper & Delivers to F&W Commission.** 

---

## **2003**

**WSC’s Status of Wild Steelhead and Their Management in Western Washington is Published** 

The Paper provides a status of wild steelhead on the West Coast with a focus on the Distinct Population Segments of Washington.

**WSC Establishes Wild Steelhead Awards** 

2 Awards established to bring attention and recognition in steelhead conservation and scholarship to promote scientific work for wild steelhead.

---

## **2004**

**Wild Steelhead Harvest Reduced From 30 per Year** 

The WSC is successful in reducing intentional annual sport harvest of wild steelhead from 30 per year to 5 per year.

**WSC Rules Proposal to Eliminate Harvest of Wild Steelhead in Olympic National Park Accepted & Implemented** 

Wild Steelhead Harvest Reduced From 5 per Year to 1 & Statewide Steelhead Mgmt. Plan 

The WSC meets with Commissioners and is finally successful in reducing intentional annual sport harvest of wild steelhead from 5 per year to 1 per year. Including mandate to develop Statewide Steelhead Management Plan which WSC participates in development.

---

## **2005**

**WSC OP/ED Piece** 

Picked up by media opposing NOAA & Bush Administration to lump hatchery steelhead the same as wild in ESA listings.

**Secure 2% for Columbia River By-Catch of ESA Listed Steelhead in Commercial Fisheries Instead of Increase to 6%** 

**Skagit River Tagging Studies for Wild Steelhead** 

WSC supports and collaborates in tagging studies in Skagit basin for wild steelhead. The project done in collaboration with WDFW, Seattle City Light, Army Corp of Engineers and Skagit Tribes. Data provides insight to steelhead migration and potential limiting factors for steelhead.

---

## **2006**

**Litigation for De-listing ESA Steelhead by Counting Hatchery Steelhead the Same As Wild** 

---

## **2007**

**WSC Proposes & Passes Regulation to Eliminate Derbies Targeting Wild Steelhead** 

**Statewide Steelhead Management Plan Development** 

WSC helps write and develop SSMP with WDFW and other stakeholders. WSC also funds two respected independent scientist to peer review the plans recommendations and provides analysis what is missing in the SSMP.

---

## **2008**

**State Wide Steelhead Management Plan Adopted** 

**Two Steelhead Summits Organized Surrounding the Topic of Wild Steelhead Gene banks and River Prospects & Work for a Wild Elwha** 

**Wild & Scenic Rivers Collaboration** 

---

## **2009**

**Early Wild Steelhead Retention Eliminated, Protections Resident Rainbow Trout** 

WSC submits & is successful in passing rules to eliminate retention of wild steelhead Nov.- Feb. to help recover early returning stocks and additional protection for resident rainbow trout.

---

## **2010**

**WSC Funds Research for Historic Wild Steelhead Abundance on Columbia River System Through Patagonia Grant** 

**WSC Organizes Steelhead Summit Topic Includes IHN Outbreak.** 

WSC organizes organizations to come together along with representatives of Congressman Norm Dicks to focus on the outbreaks of IHN disease of hatchery steelhead.

---

## **2011**

**WSC Organizes Steelhead Summit Topic: A Wild Elwha River** 

WSC brings together stakeholders hosting and sponsors SSA centering around the "Future of Native Fisheries on the Elwha" post dam removal.

**WILDSTEELHEAD New Identity** 

---

## **2012**

**WSC Joins Lawsuit Regarding Hatchery Program for Elwha** 

Litigation is filed with WFC and other partners over the use of hatchery fish for restoration of wild fish including steelhead on the Elwha River and in violation of the Elwha River Act.

---

## **2013**

**Support Developed for the Film "Wild Reverence" by Film Maker Shane Anderson** 

**Skagit Habitat Restoration** 

WSC funds habitat work for Skagit Rivers Barnaby Slough habitat work through Orvis grant to reconnect back channels. Including culvert work on Lyle Creek, tributary of Sauk River.

---

## **2014**

**Developed West coast tour for the Film "Wild Reverence"** 

WSC organizes film tour along the West coast of US with sponsorship from Patagonia and develops campaign to "Make Wild Steelhead the Priority" with focus on the Olympic Peninsula delivering over 500 signed cards to Commission.

**Wild Steelhead Gene Banks Wind, EF Lewis & NF Toutle & Green Rivers Established** 

WSC plays integral part and serves on committees to establish new wild steelhead gene bank designation.

---

## **2015**

**Steelhead Summit Alliance Topic: "A Wild Skagit River"** 

WSC brings together stakeholders hosting another SSA exploring steps needed to make the Skagit a true wild steelhead river.

**Rules Enacted to Help Restore Wild Steelhead on Olympic Peninsula** 

WSC Submits proposals to eliminate harvest of wild steelhead and selective gear rules to help reduce interactions on wild steelhead and preserve future angling opportunity.

---

## **2016**

**Keep Wild Steelhead Wet Paper** 
WSC Science Committee writes and publishes paper regarding the importance to avoid air exposure when releasing wild steelhead impacting spawning productivity.

**WSC Supports and Helps Sponsors Free the Snake Events**

**Wild Steelhead Gene Banks Established For Grays, Nisqually & Elwha Rivers** 
WSC plays integral part and serves on committees to establish new wild steelhead gene bank designations.


---

## **2017**

**Puget Sound Steelhead Advisory Group Member** 
WSC begin work with WDFW and other groups to begin craft conservation and angling opportunities portfolio in Puget Sound Distinct Individual Population (DIP).

**Rich Simms Named Conservationist of the Year** 
WSC’s Founding Board Member is named Fly Fisherman’s Magazine Sage’s first Conservationist of the Year for his tireless conservation work for wild steelhead.

**Steelhead Country Campaign & Video Series Established**

**Hoh River Sonar Support and Development** 
WSC partners to develop Hoh River Sonar to better understand run sizes using sonar technology. WSC donates equipment major funding for development.

**WSC develops a campaign series researching the hay days of Puget Sound steelhead, it’s demise and resurrection for the future of fish and anglers.**


---

## **2018**

**What Does the River Owe You?** 
WSC’s Josh Mills pens article calling out what the river and its fish owe you exploring the age of entitlement and critically depress Upper Columbia River Steelhead.

**WSC Provides Thompson River Leadership** 
WSC develops fundraising campaign to support extremely depress stocks of Thompson River wild steelhead in British Columbia.


---

## **2019**

**Olympic Peninsula Law Enforcement** 
WSC provides WDFW Law Enforcement much needed equipment to enforce the laws and stop poaching on Olympic Peninsula Rivers.

**Grande Ronde Access Purchase Campaign** 
WSC working to secure funding for public access for the benefit of fish and anglers to be held in public trust in perpetuity.

**WSC Works with Grays Harbor Sport fishing Coalition** 
WSC and Grays Harbor Sport fishing Coalition work together with WDFW to implement Selective Gear rules for the upper Humptulips River.

**WSC Funds Research for Historic Wild Steelhead Abundance on Olympic Peninsula.**


---

## **2020**

**Olympic Peninsula Wild Steelhead Trajectory/Concerns** 
WSC pens article to bing attention to steelhead trajectory on the OP and calling for collaborative efforts to work together to make changes before they go to potential, future listing.

**Chehalis Dam Campaign Partnership** 
WSC provides major partnership and leadership in prevention of Chehalis River Dam proposal.

**WSC Puget Sound Steelhead Advisory Group Quick Silver Portfolio** 
QuickSilver Portfolio is released after three years of work with diverse member providing a future vision for steelhead in Puget Sound region.

## **2021**

**WILD STEELHEAD NOW OR NEVER CAMPAIGN INTRODUCED** 
In 2021, being a dedicated wild steelhead angler means fishing through a paradox. While the enthusiasm for chasing these beautiful, powerful fish is at an all-time high, there have never been fewer wild steelhead returning to their home waters.

**20 YEARS of WILD STEELHEAD ADVOCACY**


---

## **2022**

**Wild Steelhead Education** 
Produced numerous educational blogs, Social Media posts, including articles in various publications and introduced a puzzle piece concept to help understand all the issues facing wild steelhead.

**Skagit River Steelhead Research** 
Funded and initiated research on how the dispersal of fry and distribution of spawning redds interact to shape density dependence in the Skagit River.

**"Steelhead In Our Hands"** 
In collaboration with Keep Fish Wet, a grassroots effort to empower anglers to protect wild steelhead through science based best handling practices.

**Adipose Podcast Introduced** 
Providing an immersive medium as a place for critical, authentic conversations that educate and inspire anglers, members of the public, and the fishing industry to act on behalf of wild steelhead.


---

## **2023**

**Amplified Conservation Voices** 
Through our support and partnership of the Osprey, we’ve helped bring critical scientific research and policy discussions on wild salmon and steelhead conservation to the forefront.

**Political Advocacy**
We’ve been a voice for Wild Steelhead in state and federal government halls, pushing for policies prioritizing their long term recovery.

**Innovative Water-Sharing Solutions**
Our financial gift to the Deschutes River Conservancy helped in their efforts to establish water-sharing agreements that are crucial for the survival of Wild Steelhead in the Deschutes Basin.

**Protecting Resident Rainbow Trout**
Filed a petition and led a campaign in Washington State, sparking a directive from the Washington Wildlife Commission to develop and implement a statewide wild trout policy.
